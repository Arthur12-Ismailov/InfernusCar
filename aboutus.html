Our Story

Founded in 1990 in the bustling city of San Francisco by visionary entrepreneur Mel Litster, Infenus Car began its journey with a dream to revolutionize the automobile industry. The early years were fraught with challenges, and by 1995, the company was on the verge of bankruptcy. Yet, through resilience and innovative strategies, Mel Litster managed to steer the company away from collapse, setting the stage for its meteoric rise.

By the year 2000, Infenus Car had become the most popular car dealership in San Francisco, expanding its presence across nearly all states in the United States. The company continued to grow exponentially, and by 2010, it had solidified its position as the wealthiest automotive company, boasting an impressive revenue of $100 billion.

Today, Infenus Car is a powerhouse in the automobile industry, proudly collaborating with world-renowned companies such as Tesla, Mercedes-Benz, Toyota, and Samsung. This legacy of excellence continues under the leadership of Arthurio Litster, son of the esteemed founder, Mel Litster. Arthurio has taken the company to even greater heights, ensuring that Infenus Car remains at the forefront of innovation and customer satisfaction.

At Infenus Car, we believe in providing our customers with the best selection of vehicles, unparalleled service, and a commitment to excellence that has defined us for over three decades.
